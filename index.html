Our Story

Based in Portarlington, Co. Laois, TheGardenShop.ie was founded in 2011 with a mission to provide a vast range of garden products at the best value in Ireland. With a low-cost approach and a strong emphasis on customer service, we quickly became a popular choice in the Irish gardening market. Our online store caters to everyone, from novice gardeners to seasoned professionals, offering a wide selection of tools and supplies.

As the only discount online garden centre in Ireland, TheGardenShop.ie aims to break the traditional pricing models, bringing high-quality products at competitive prices. Today, we offer over 2500 products, including trusted brands like Felco, Wolf Garden, and Wilkinson Sword. Our dedicated team continuously seeks out top-tier tools, plants, and gardening essentials for our customers.

In 2016, we upgraded our website to enhance the shopping experience, and in 2021, we launched SOLAS, an eco-friendly garden shop and food village, providing sustainable gardening solutions alongside our full range of plants, seeds, and accessories.
